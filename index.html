Biography

Octavious is an American Shorthair cat. He was born in a barn outside of Pittsburgh, PA. He was immediatley adopted by loving parents who take great care of him. He also goes by Kitty, Pretty Kitty, Octavious Catto, Fatty, and Jabba the Cat. He likes to take long naps during the day. He is also a very talkative cat. He communicates through various Meows and Peeps.
